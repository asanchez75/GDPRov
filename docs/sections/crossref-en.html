DataAnonymisationStepc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: http://purl.org/adaptcentre/openscience/ontologies/gdprov#DataAnonymisationStep

DataAnonymisationStep anonymises data by transforming it from one form to another along the anonymisation chain. Anonymisation can be represented as a spectrum going from raw user data to pseudo-anonymised data that can be de-anonymised by the same agent/organisation to pseudo-anonymous data that cannot be deanonymised internally, but may be done by external agents who have access to other data, and finally to completely anonymised data.
has super-classes
DataTransformationStep c
is in domain of
generatesAnonymisedData op

Anonymisedni back to ToC or Named Individual ToC

IRI: http://purl.org/adaptcentre/openscience/ontologies/gdprov#Anonymised

Anonymised represents the Anonymisation level where the data cannot be de-anonymised to retrieve personally identifiable information.

DeAnonymisedni back to ToC or Named Individual ToC

IRI: http://purl.org/adaptcentre/openscience/ontologies/gdprov#DeAnonymised

DeAnonymised represents the Anonymisation level where the data is completely de-anonymised and contains directly accessible personally identifiable information.

PseudoAnonymisedni back to ToC or Named Individual ToC

IRI: http://purl.org/adaptcentre/openscience/ontologies/gdprov#PseudoAnonymised

PseudoAnonymised represents the Anonymisation level where the data is anonymised but cannot be de-anonymised without additional data which is NOT accessible to the data-holding organisation to retrieve personally identifiable information.

PseudoOrganisationalOrganisedni back to ToC or Named Individual ToC

IRI: http://purl.org/adaptcentre/openscience/ontologies/gdprov#PseudoOrganisationalAnonymised

PseudoOrganisationalAnonymised represents the Anonymisation level where the data is anonymised but cannot be de-anonymised without additional data which is accessible to the data-holding organisation to recreate the de-anonymised information.
